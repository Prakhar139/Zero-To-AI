============================================================
  ZERO TO AI — SHORT #1 SCRIPT
  "You're using ChatGPT WRONG"
  Language: Hinglish | Duration: ~50 seconds
  Type: YouTube Short (9:16 vertical)
============================================================

-------------------------------------------
[0-2s] HOOK — Close-up face, serious look
-------------------------------------------
"Tum ChatGPT galat use kar rahe ho."
(Point at camera)

-------------------------------------------
[2-5s] THE PROBLEM — Cut to screen recording
-------------------------------------------
"90% log ChatGPT mein seedha type karte hain..."
(Show typing on screen)
"Write me an email to my boss for leave."

-------------------------------------------
[5-8s] BAD OUTPUT — Show ChatGPT's boring response
-------------------------------------------
"Aur ChatGPT deta hai... yeh generic, boring email."
(Scroll through the output)
"Koi bhi pakad lega — yeh toh AI ne likha hai bhai."

-------------------------------------------
[8-12s] THE SECRET — Face to camera, slow & confident
-------------------------------------------
"Lekin ek line add karo... sirf EK line..."
(Pause for effect)
"PEHLE ChatGPT ko batao — KON hai woh."

-------------------------------------------
[12-18s] SHOW THE FIX — Screen recording, type slowly
-------------------------------------------
(Type this on screen, word by word)
"You are a professional communication expert 
who writes crisp, human-sounding emails."

(Then below it, type the same question)
"Write me an email to my boss for 2 days leave."

-------------------------------------------
[18-25s] GOOD OUTPUT — Screen recording, wow moment
-------------------------------------------
"Ab dekho... SAME question..."
(Scroll through new output)
"Lekin email COMPLETELY different hai."
"Professional. Natural. Lagta hi nahi AI ne likha."

-------------------------------------------
[25-30s] SIDE BY SIDE — Split screen comparison
-------------------------------------------
(Left side: bad output | Right side: good output)
"Yeh WITHOUT role... yeh WITH role."
"Same ChatGPT. Same question."
"Zameen-aasmaan ka farak."

-------------------------------------------
[30-38s] EXPLAIN — Face to camera, excited tone
-------------------------------------------
"Yaad rakho — ChatGPT ek ACTOR hai."
"Agar tum role nahi doge..."
"toh woh generic perform karega."
"Role do... toh EXPERT ban jaayega."

-------------------------------------------
[38-45s] BONUS — Quick text flashes on screen
-------------------------------------------
(Flash each for 1.5 seconds with voiceover)

"Try karo yeh roles:"

→ "You are a fitness coach"
   — diet plan maango

→ "You are a stand-up comedian"  
   — funny content banwao

→ "You are an IIT professor"
   — koi bhi topic samjho

-------------------------------------------
[45-50s] CTA — Face to camera, point & smile
-------------------------------------------
"Bas EK line se poora game change."
"Follow karo aur aisi AI tricks daily paao."
"Aur yeh video apne us dost ko bhejo..."
"jo abhi bhi ChatGPT se boring answers le raha hai."
(Smile + point at camera)

============================================================
  END OF SCRIPT
